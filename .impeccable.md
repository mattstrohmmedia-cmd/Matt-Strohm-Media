## Design Context

### Users
Creative clients in Aberdeen & Aberdeenshire — primarily engaged couples, local businesses, and brands seeking photography, videography, web design, social media, and AI services. They discover Matt via word of mouth or social media, often comparing him against other local photographers/videographers. Decision context: browsing on both mobile and desktop, looking for trust signals, portfolio quality, and a sense of the person behind the work.

### Brand Personality
Sleek, modern, cinematic. Matt is a solo creative who operates at the intersection of traditional craft (weddings, events) and forward-thinking services (AI, web). The brand should feel like a premium Edinburgh or London agency that happens to be rooted in Scotland — confident, not flashy.

Three words: **Cinematic. Confident. Considered.**

### Aesthetic Direction
- Dark theme: near-black (#050505) with warm-tinted gold accent (#c9a84c)
- Editorial feel — think Kinfolk meets a modern agency showreel
- Typography: Instrument Serif (display) + Syne (body) — already in use
- Motion: award-winning studio energy. Clip-path wipes, dramatic scroll reveals, parallax. Nothing cheap or bouncy.
- Anti-reference: generic WordPress photography templates, anything with a white background and pastel colors

### Design Principles
1. **Imagery is the hero** — every layout decision should frame and elevate the photography/video
2. **Motion with purpose** — every animation either reveals, transitions, or provides feedback. Nothing decorative for its own sake.
3. **Cinematic pacing** — entrances are deliberate and confident, not instant or choppy
4. **Gold is rare** — the accent color appears only where it genuinely earns attention
5. **One voice** — the site feels like a single, coherent creative vision from top to bottom
