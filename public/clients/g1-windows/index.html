Onboarding Questionnaire
15 mins
Answer however much you like — bullet points are fine. Hit the button at the bottom to email me your answers.
01 · The Business
In one paragraph, how would you describe G1 to a friend who'd never heard of you? What makes G1 different from every other window cleaner in Glasgow?
02 · Customers
Who are your best customers? Residential or commercial? Specific areas of Glasgow? Any types of jobs you want more of (or less of)?
03 · Tone
How do you want to come across online? Trustworthy and professional? Friendly and local? Premium? Pick three words.
04 · Look & Feel
Any colours you like or hate? Any brands (window cleaning or otherwise) whose look you admire? Anything from the existing G1 brand you want to keep?
05 · Competitors
Who do you see as your main competitors in Glasgow? Anyone doing something on social you've noticed (good or bad)?
Links to their Facebook / Instagram if you have them handy.
06 · Services To Highlight
Which services do you make the most margin on, or want more of? (e.g. residential regulars, gutter cleans, commercial contracts, end-of-tenancy)
07 · Trust Signals
Beyond the #1 ThreeBestRated badge and 12 years trading — any other reviews, accreditations, insurance details, or memberships you want featured?
08 · Content Comfort
Are you happy being on camera occasionally (just phone clips of the work — face on camera is optional)? Any team members up for a quick clip now and then?
09 · Phone & Enquiries
Best phone number and email for enquiries to go to? Do you want a quote form on the site, a direct WhatsApp link, or both?
10 · Anything Else
Anything I haven't asked that I should know? Things to avoid, sensitivities, ideas you've had, things you've always wanted to try?
